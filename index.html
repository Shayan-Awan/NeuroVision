About NeuroVision

NeuroVision is an open-source brainwave-powered vision enhancement device designed to reduce symptoms in patients suffering from Irlen syndrome. Utilizing EEG sensors, NeuroVision allows users to swap through optical filters on a pair of goggles, providing customized visual experiences.

Features

  • Variety of color options
  • Variety of transparency options
  • Support on mac only (as of now)
  • Open-source and community-driven
